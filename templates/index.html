Hi there! My name is Almond, and I like messing around with computers.

I'm an undergrad at Grinnell College majoring in Computer Science, but also dabbling in pure math, linguistics, theater, and anything else that catches my attention. In order of my favorites, I'm comfortable programming in C, Haskell, Python, and Java.

You may know me as that nerd who uses Linux on their personal computer for the hell of it, and you won't be hard pressed to find me pulling up the terminal to try my hand at bash wizardry.

When it comes to computing, I have two major interests: sysadmin and peer education. Are these things very different? Yes. But working on both of them brings me a lot of joy, and I don't see myself stopping anytime soon.

My favorite computer relatex activities include tinkering with various projects, manager, playing Celeste, and breaking my window manager every other week. My favorite NON-computer related activities, on the other hand, include hiking, reading (mostly mystery and scifi), and coming up with new words to confuse my family and friends with.
