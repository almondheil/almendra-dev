Hi there! My name is almond, and I like messing around with computers.

I'm an undergrad at Grinnell College majoring in Computer Science, but also dabbling in pure math, linguistics, theater, and anything else that catches my attention. In order of my favorites, I'm comfortable programming in C, Python, Haskell, and Java.

I'm also that nerd who uses linux on its personal laptop (surely widespread adoption can't be that far off, right?), and tries to use `awk`, `grep`, and a whole host of other CLI tools whenever they can get away with it.

When it comes to computing, I'm really fascinated by system administration--it lends itself wonderfully to my enjoyment of the command line, and I find a lot of fun in troubleshooting issues that arise on the systems that I manage. Sure, sometimes it can be really annoying to update all your configuration and services to a new domain name, but it's worth it in the end and a fun challenge along the way.

I also love working in peer education for CS, and just helping folks understand code in general. I've been able to participate in it a lot more thanks to being a TA at Grinnell, but even outside of school I find a lot of value in working with people on their projects--I learn a lot about what they're doing, and hopefully they learn more about how to code it! It's a win-win.

My favorite computer related activities include tinkering with various projects, manager, playing Celeste, and breaking my window manager every other week. My favorite NON-computer related activities, on the other hand, include hiking, reading (mostly mystery and scifi), and coming up with new words to confuse my family and friends with.
